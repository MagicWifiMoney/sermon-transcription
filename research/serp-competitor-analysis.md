# Sermon Transcription SERP & Competitor Analysis

**Report Date:** February 13, 2026  
**Analysis Type:** Complete SERP competitor research + domain availability

---

## Executive Summary

The sermon transcription market is **moderately competitive but highly fragmented**, split between:
- **AI-first pure players** (TurboScribe, PrismaScribe, ScreenApp) offering low-cost automated transcription
- **Human transcription services** (Intentional Reach, GMR Transcription) positioning as premium/accurate
- **Content repurposing platforms** (Sermon Shots, Pulpit AI) that bundle transcription into larger suites
- **Church software add-ons** (Proclaim/Logos, SermonAudio) where transcription is a feature, not the product

**Key finding:** No single player dominates. Most rank for only 1-2 of our target keywords, suggesting **opportunity for a focused, SEO-optimized pure transcription service**.

**Available premium domains:** `transcribesermons.com`, `sermontotext.com`, `sermontoblog.com`, `sermonwords.com`
